The Stable

This historical building has recently given a new lease of life in Easter 2018 and is now available as a holiday let.

Large stable door for entering.

Living room

Upstairs living room with Juliette balcony to listen to the birds singing in the trees.

Kitchen

Kitchen/dining room with bi-fold doors to open to patio area, comfortable sofa and TV.

Bedroom

Deluxe beds, pocket springs, toppers and cotton linen.
