Guest Book

If you would like to predict how good your stay at Hawley Farm will be without any perceived marketing bias of this website, then please look at our feedback forms and visitor book which some visitors fill in at the end of their stay. Any names, addresses or phone numbers have been blurred for conservation of anonymity. Or, you can view us on Trip Advisor by clicking one of: Cider Press or Wagon House.



We would appreciate anybody that has stayed at Hawley Farm to give comments below for the rest of the world to see. Please also send us your photos that you would not mind being added to this website.
