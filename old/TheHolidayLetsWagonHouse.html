Wagon House



Overview

  • 2 en-suite bedrooms sleeping 4
  • Large glass facade to living room & bedroom 1
  • Under floor heating on both floors
  • Woodburning stove
  • Sun loungers and garden furniture
  • Sunny location

Entrance

You enter the "Wagon House" via a graveled/paved area with garden furniture and sun loungers, a perfect morning sun trap. There are flower borders around, up 5 steps and you enter another graveled garden with flower tubs. A BBQ is available.

Ground floor

From the first garden you enter though double glass doors into a spacious living/dining/ kitchen room. There is under floor heating throughout with a wood burner for those autumn /winter breaks to sit around on a comfortable leather suite with freeview TV/DVD for your enjoyment. The dining area has an oak table with 4 chairs. The kitchen is well equiped with dishwasher, fridge with freezer box, single oven, hob, microwave, toaster & kettle. All the crockery and cooking utensils are provided for 4 people. A door leads off to a utility area with a washing machine and tumble dryer. There is a WC on the ground floor.

Bedrooms

Up the stairs half way there is a door leading outside to a graveled area. At the top of the stairs there is a landing to the oak floor bedrooms. Bedroom 1 - has views of farm & countryside a 6' bed , bedroom furniture, TV /DVD . The large window allows a stream of sunlight in the morning. The en-suite bathroom has a shower over the bath. Bedroom 2 - has a double (5ft) or 2 x 2'6'' singles. The en-suite bathroom has a large shower.
