Reviews

If you would like to predict how good your stay at Hawley Farm will be without any perceived marketing bias of this website, then please look at our

  • Reviews on Trip Advisor by clicking one of: Cider Press, Wagon House or Stables. Note however that it is beneficial to book direct, through this website.
  • Reviews on Booking.com. Note however that it is beneficial to book direct, through this website.
  • Feedback forms and visitor book which some visitors fill in at the end of their stay. Any names, addresses or phone numbers have been blurred for conservation of anonymity. See below.


We would appreciate anybody that has stayed at Hawley Farm to give comments below for the rest of the world to see. Please also send us your photos that you would not mind being added to this website.

Stevens Family said:
We have just returned from a week staying in the Cider Press.
It is a lovely cottage - spotlessly clean, well furnished & in such a lovely spot. It was so enjoyable just to sit at the table and watch what was happening outside - & there was always something going on. Its a nice walk across the fields to the village of Dalwood. We would suggest that if you want to eat at the village pub (The Tuckers Arms) that you do book a table. We would also recommend the River Cottage Canteen in Axminster. All in all a very relaxing and entertaining week & certainly a cottage we would recommend to friends and family.

The Mason Family said:
We would all like to thank you for the lovely holiday. We stayed in the Cider Press. The highlight of the week was the tour around the Farm. The accommodation was 1st class,as were the scones. We have recommended you to our freinds, and hope to stay with you again soon

Smith Family said:
My daughter booked Cider Press Cottage as a surprise weekend for his 60th birthday and couldnt have made a better choice! The six of us were made very welcome and all our immediate needs were provided tea bags provided and milk in the fridge not to mention the delicious complimentary cream tea! Everything you might need is here and plenty more besides. The local pub is within walking distance and serves good food. We only wish we had been able to spend longer here we definitely need to arrange a return visit. Thoroughly recommended.
